Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

HIIT & Tabata ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by HIIT & Tabata. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly. The app operates primarily with data stored locally on your device.

  • Workout records: Your workout history (duration, configuration, date) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.
  • App settings: Your preferences (sound mode, colors, language) are stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can limit ad tracking in your iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
Google Privacy Policy →

Firebase Analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used (e.g., feature usage, session length). This data is aggregated and anonymized and does not identify you personally.
Firebase Privacy Policy →

Firebase Crashlytics
We use Firebase Crashlytics to receive crash reports. Crash reports may include device model, OS version, and the state of the app at the time of the crash. No personal data is included.
Firebase Privacy Policy →

3. Data Retention

Locally stored workout records and settings remain on your device until you delete the app. Third-party analytics and crash data are retained per each provider's own policies.

4. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
